This card set was made for my Visual Foundation Studio course as an exercise in modularity, gestalt principles, and hierarchical design.

In the spirit of the Bauhaus, pips representing card suit were replaced with a circle (♠), triangle (♣), square (♦), and organic gestural design (♥). Design elements were limited to these and no type was used.
